Hi, I'm Hannah Nelson!


I am currently studying a Studio Arts BA and will be graduating in December of 2021 from the California State University of Channel Islands. I specialize in 3D environmental animation, digital illustration and website design.
Previous to studying at California State University Channel Islands I worked in the IT field for 7 years. My love for IT and technical challenges is what lead me to the field of digital art. I felt that my creative roots, strong attention to detail, and technical talents could be better used in a more creative field such as; gaming or design.
Through the pursuit of my bachelor's degree I have found that I have great passion for designing; 3D modelling, web design and digital illustration have become my dearest tools - but more specifically environmental nature and assets that reside within video games.
I have long played video games and as a result feel I can contribute insight and ideas due to many years of careful observation.
